## **THE 7 QUESTIONS**

*Scoring guide at the end — each answer maps to one of 4 result types: Audience, Format, Offer, or Follow-Up*

---

### **Q1. Who did you have in mind when you created your lead magnet?**

A) A very specific type of person at a specific stage in their business or life *(Offer/Format)* B) Broadly, anyone who might be interested in what I do *(Audience)* C) Honestly, I was thinking about what I could create, not who it was for *(Audience)* D) My existing audience — people who already follow me *(Follow-Up)*

---

### **Q2. How does your lead magnet connect to your paid offer?**

A) It's a direct stepping stone — it solves one part of the problem my paid offer solves completely *(Format/Offer — positive signal)* B) It's related, but the link isn't super explicit *(Offer)* C) It's a bit of a different topic, but I thought it would get more sign-ups *(Audience)* D) I'm not totally sure — I haven't mapped it out properly *(Offer)*

---

### **Q3. What format is your lead magnet in?**

A) A PDF guide, checklist, or ebook *(Format)* B) A video or mini training *(Format)* C) A template or swipe file *(Format — positive signal)* D) A quiz, assessment, or interactive tool *(Format — strong positive signal)*

---

### **Q4. What happens immediately after someone signs up?**

A) They get the lead magnet delivered and a welcome email sequence kicks in *(Follow-Up — positive signal)* B) They get the lead magnet, but there's no real email sequence after that *(Follow-Up)* C) They get the lead magnet. I email my list occasionally but it's not automated *(Follow-Up)* D) Honestly, the delivery is a bit patchy — I'm not sure it always works properly *(Follow-Up)*

---

### **Q5. When you look at your sign-up page, what does the traffic look like?**

A) Good traffic, low conversions — people are landing but not signing up *(Format/Offer)* B) Low traffic — not many people are even finding the page *(Audience)* C) Decent sign-ups, but nobody's buying anything afterwards *(Follow-Up)* D) I don't really have a clear picture of the numbers *(mixed signal — defaults to Offer)*

---

### **Q6. How would you describe the people who do sign up?**

A) They're exactly who I want to work with *(Follow-Up — the breakdown is after sign-up)* B) A mixed bag — some good fits, a lot of people who'll never buy *(Audience)* C) I'm not sure — I don't hear from them much after they sign up *(Follow-Up)* D) Mostly people who like free stuff but aren't serious buyers *(Audience/Offer)*

---

### **Q7. When someone gets your lead magnet, what are they supposed to do next?**

A) There's a clear next step — a specific offer, a call to book, a product to buy *(positive signal)* B) Get value from it, and hopefully reach out when they're ready *(Follow-Up)* C) Join my email list and hear from me over time *(Follow-Up)* D) Honestly, I haven't been that intentional about it *(Offer)*

---

## **SCORING LOGIC**

Tally which letter category appears most across their 7 answers:

| Result | When to show it |
| :---- | :---- |
| **Wrong Audience** | Majority of B/C answers on Q1, Q2, Q5, Q6 |
| **Wrong Format** | Majority of A/B answers on Q3 \+ Q5, with mixed elsewhere |
| **Wrong Offer** | Majority of B/C/D answers on Q2, Q7, Q5 — the join-up between lead magnet and paid offer is weak |
| **Wrong Follow-Up** | Majority of D answers on Q4, C on Q5, A on Q6, B/C on Q7 |

*Note for GHL build: set up weighted scoring with each answer option tagged to a result type. The result with the highest score wins. In a tie between Offer and Follow-Up, default to Follow-Up.*
